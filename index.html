Introduction

As college students, we face constant pressure in balancing work, physical well-being, and mental health, with nutrition being one of the most vital factors affecting our overall well-being. Proper nutrition plays a key role in enhancing a student's educational experience. As a result, to many students, it is important to know how well insitutions they either currently attend or potentially will prioritizes their well being and nutrition.

With this in mind, we seek to examine the relationship between food quality ratings and factors such as tuition, geographical location, and student population. By uncovering the trends between these factors, we seek to uncover the key drivers of student satisfaction and regarding their nutrition offerings.

The insights from this analysis will shed light on how numerous important variables correlate with overall student dining satisfaction. This will provide a deeper understanding of what factors indicate a school will be doing well, and help institutions identify areas for improvement, ensuring students make the most of their higher education experience.
